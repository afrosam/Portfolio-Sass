BIO

Hello, and nice to meet you! My name is Sam Trettin. I am a passionate self taught developer with over 7 years experience. I enjoy working out, relaxing with friends, building things, and solving problems. Programming to me is the answer to questions you have yet to be asked. It just takes a little know how and creativity and you can get it done. I got into programming while checking out W3C on my lunch break at my prior job as a Machinist/TIG welder. I always liked building things but enjoyed it more with a skosh of science added to it. So programming seemed interesting. While checking out PHP and running tests and connecting the dots I decided to get into it full on. A few months later I landed my first job at Virtual Graffiti.

In my time as a developer I have built many websites, custom styles, ensured responsiveness, enhanced security, ran CRUD operations in various databases, automated workflow, built APIs, set up email campaigns, built data visualizations, and created QA tests.

No matter what I am doing, I try and do the best I can to get the job done as quickly, safely, and with the best results as possible.
